Physics-Driven Peripheral Simulation

Beyond register mocking: Simulate real electrical behaviors.

LabWired models the analog reality of hardware. From I2C pull-up resizing to ADC noise injection, our agents interact with a simulation that pushes back, ensuring your firmware handles the real world, not just the happy path.

Integrated with standard SVD files and extensible via Python, LabWired lets you define complex component interactions—sensors, actuators, and displays—creating a complete virtual lab bench for your AI agents.

Agent-First Debugging Interface

A debugger designed for AI, not just humans.

Traditional GDB is great for humans, but agents need structured observability. LabWired exposes memory, registers, and peripheral states via a high-performance gRPC API, allowing autonomous agents to inspect, step, and correct firmware in real-time.
